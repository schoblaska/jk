## Legal Standing

- **Noah Cross as guardian**: As Evelyn's father and Katherine's grandfather (in the public record), Cross has the strongest familial claim to custody. No competing relatives exist. [Hollis Mulwray](../h1m0) was not Katherine's biological or legal father.

The evidence of his crime against Evelyn exists only as testimony from Evelyn (now deceased) relayed through Gittes (a private investigator, not a legal party).

## Who Could Act?
tags: #legal

- **Jake Gittes**: No legal standing. Not a family member, not a court officer. His testimony is hearsay.

- **LAPD / DA**: Would need to open a criminal investigation into Cross. The [legal avenues analysis](l1a0) already assessed this as effectively impossible.
- **A sympathetic judge**: Family court could appoint a guardian ad litem for Katherine, but the petition would need to come from someone with standing, and Cross's attorneys would fight it.
