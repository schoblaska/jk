## Phase 1: Preparation (January–March 1937)
tags: #water, #property

- **January**: [LADWP water diversion](w1d0) begins - reservoir gates opened during off-hours, water discharged through ocean outfalls at night. Logged as "maintenance" with no corresponding work orders.
- **January–March**: Wells in the northwest San Fernando Valley begin failing. Ranchers report contamination. [Oak Pass Reservoir](../o1p0) water quality deteriorates.
- **February**: [Property purchases](p1t0) begin in the northwest valley. 47 parcels acquired over six months through [Albacore Club](../a1c0) members and their proxies at 30–50% below assessed value.
- **March**: [Alto Vallejo Dam](../a1v0) proposal formally introduced to city council. [Hollis Mulwray](../h1m0) publicly opposes it, citing geological concerns and the [Van der Lip precedent](v1d0).

## Phase 2: Discrediting Mulwray (June 1937)

- **June 21**: [Ida Sessions](../i1s0), posing as Mrs. Mulwray, hires Jake Gittes to surveil Hollis Mulwray. Purpose: create photographic evidence of an apparent affair to destroy Mulwray's credibility.
- **June 23**: Gittes photographs Mulwray with [Katherine](../k1c0) at Echo Park. Simultaneously, a separate photographer captures the same scene. Photos appear on the [front page of the LA Times](l1t0) before Gittes can deliver them to his client. The leak was [pre-arranged](l1t0).
- **June 23**: [Evelyn Mulwray](../e1c0) confronts Gittes. The real Mrs. Mulwray never hired him. The setup is exposed, but the public damage to Mulwray is done.

## Phase 3: Murder (June 25–27)
tags: #murder

- **June 25**: Mulwray continues investigating water diversion, visiting drainage channels and ocean outfalls at night.
- **June 25–26 (overnight)**: Mulwray is killed - drowned in [salt water](../s1w0) near the Point Fermin outfall, likely between 10 PM and 2 AM based on [tidal analysis](t1a0). Body transported to [Oak Pass Reservoir](../o1p0) and staged as an accidental drowning.
- **June 27**: Body discovered. LAPD rules it accidental. [Lt. Escobar](../l1e0) warns Gittes to stay away. [Claude Mulvihill](../c1m0) - Cross's man at LADWP - threatens Gittes at the reservoir.

## Phase 4: Consolidation (Late June–Present)

- **June 30**: [Noah Cross](../n1c0) meets with Gittes. Asks him to find [Katherine](../k1c0). Offers money. Cross is positioning to take custody of the girl now that Mulwray - her protector - is dead.
- **June 30**: [Albacore Club](../a1c0) membership [cross-referenced](a1x0) with property deeds - near-complete overlap confirms the land syndicate.
- **July 2**: Evelyn reveals Katherine's true parentage. Cross is Katherine's father and grandfather. The conspiracy is personal as well as financial.
