# What I Know
date: [Sat, Jul 03 1937](1937-07-03)
tags: #summary

Everything I pieced together. All of it true. None of it enough.

**The conspiracy:**
[Noah Cross](n1c0) engineered a [drought](d1r0) by diverting [LADWP water](ai/w1d0) through ocean outfalls and poisoning valley wells. While the northwest San Fernando Valley dried up, his [Albacore Club](a1c0) associates bought [47 parcels of farmland](ai/p1t0) through shell companies at distressed prices. The [Alto Vallejo Dam](a1v0) - pushed through city council - will send public water to Cross's private land. The [full timeline](ai/t1c0) documents every step.

**The murder:**
[Hollis Mulwray](h1m0) opposed the dam and was investigating the water diversion. Cross had him [discredited](ai/l1t0) through [Ida Sessions](i1s0) and then [killed](s1w0) - drowned in salt water at the coast and dumped in [Oak Pass Reservoir](o1p0). [Claude Mulvihill](c1m0), Cross's man at LADWP, handled enforcement. [Tidal analysis](ai/t1a0) confirms the timeline. [Escobar](l1e0) was pressured to close it as accidental.

**The family:**
Cross assaulted his daughter [Evelyn](e1c0) when she was fifteen. [Katherine](k1c0) is the child. Mulwray married Evelyn and helped hide Katherine from Cross. Cross wanted Katherine back. With Mulwray dead, Evelyn was the last barrier.

**The outcome:**
Evelyn is dead. Shot in [Chinatown](c1t0) trying to get Katherine out of the city. Cross took Katherine. The police did nothing. The [legal analysis](ai/l1a0) says no one will prosecute. The DA is bought, the cops are leashed, the feds don't care.

**What's left:**
This notebook. Every note, every link, every AI analysis. A [complete archive](ai/c1a0) of a conspiracy that will make one man richer and everyone else poorer. A record, in case it ever matters to someone with more power than I have.

It matters to me. That's not enough. But it's what I've got.
