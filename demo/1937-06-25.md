## Mulwray's Movements

[Evelyn](e1c0) hired me for real this time - find out who set them both up. So I'm back on [Mulwray](h1m0), but now I'm watching him differently. Not looking for a mistress. Looking for what he's looking for.

He drove out to the LA River at night. Not the scenic part - the concrete drainage channels east of the city. Took measurements, wrote in a notebook. Then out to the ocean outfall near Point Fermin. Stood there watching freshwater pour into the Pacific.

During a drought. The city is rationing water, and thousands of gallons are being dumped into the ocean after dark.

## Oak Pass Reservoir

Visited the [reservoir](o1p0) during the day. Talked to a kid who said the water "tastes funny." Local ranchers nearby say their wells have gone bad - livestock won't drink. One old-timer said it started about six months ago, right when the [drought](d1r0) got serious.

Six months. Same timeframe the AI flagged for the [valley land purchases](ai/p1t0).

## Theory

The [drought is engineered](d1r0). Someone is deliberately wasting water - dumping it at the outfalls, poisoning wells in the valley - to manufacture a crisis. If the valley dries up, the farmers go broke. Broke farmers sell cheap.

Then the [Alto Vallejo Dam](a1v0) gets built with public money, water flows to the valley, and whoever bought all that [cheap land](l1g0) watches their investment multiply tenfold.

Mulwray figured it out. That's why he's out there at midnight measuring water flow. He's not a cheating husband - he's a whistleblower who hasn't blown the whistle yet. And someone needed to [discredit him](ai/l1t0) before he could.
