# Alto Vallejo Dam
date: [Mon, Jun 21 1937](1937-06-21)
tags: #place, #water

Proposed reservoir project in the northwest San Fernando Valley. City council is pushing hard to get it approved. Big money behind it - development interests want the water infrastructure in place.

[Hollis Mulwray](h1m0) is publicly opposed. Says the site geology can't support it. Pointed to the [Van der Lip Dam disaster](ai/v1d0) as precedent - same kind of sandstone, same kind of hubris.

Council doesn't want to hear it. They've got votes lined up.

Question is: who benefits if the dam gets built and the water flows to the valley?
