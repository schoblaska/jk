# Ida Sessions
date: [Wed, Jun 23 1937](1937-06-23)
tags: #person, #suspect

The fake Mrs. Mulwray. Hired me under false pretenses to tail [Hollis Mulwray](h1m0) and catch him with [the girl](t1g0). Played the part well - good clothes, steady voice, knew enough details to be convincing.

Someone put her up to it. She's not the brains - she's the face. An actress, maybe, or someone who owed a favor. The kind of person you use once and throw away.

Questions:
- Who hired her to hire me?
- How did she know the details about Mulwray's routine?
- Where did the [LA Times photos](ai/l1t0) come from if not from me?

Need to find her. She's the thread that connects the setup to whoever's pulling the strings.
