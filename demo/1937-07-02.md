# Fri, Jul 02 1937
tags: #journal

## The Girl's Name

Katherine. Found her at a house [Evelyn](e1c0) keeps in the valley - small place, curtains always drawn, a housekeeper who didn't want to let me in. [The girl](t1g0) was inside. Scared, quiet, barely spoke above a whisper. She's been hidden her whole life.

Evelyn has been keeping her here. Away from everyone. Away from [her father](n1c0).

## "She's My Daughter"

Went back to Evelyn. I pushed. Harder than I should have. She broke.

"She's my sister." Then silence. Then again, different. "She's my daughter." Then crying. Then both at once. "She's my sister *and* my daughter."

[Noah Cross assaulted his own daughter](k1c0). Katherine is the product. Evelyn was fifteen. Her mother was already dead. There was no one to protect her and no one to tell.

Mulwray knew. He married Evelyn knowing everything. He helped her hide Katherine, gave the girl a life away from Cross. That's what the photos at Echo Park were - not an affair. A father visiting his wife's child. Protecting her the only way he knew how.

Everything Evelyn has done - the privacy, the [quiet philanthropy for orphanages](ai/e1b0), the distance from the Cross name - it all makes sense now. She's been running from her father for twenty years.

## Everything Connects

[Cross](n1c0) didn't just want Mulwray out of the way because of the [dam](a1v0). Mulwray was the barrier between Cross and [Katherine](k1c0). With Mulwray dead and Evelyn alone, Cross can take what he wants. He always takes what he wants.

The [whole scheme](s1f0) is one machine. The [drought](d1r0), the [land](l1g0), the [dam](a1v0), the [scandal](ai/l1t0), the [murder](s1w0). Cross engineers every piece. [Ida Sessions](i1s0) was hired to discredit Mulwray publicly. [Mulvihill](c1m0) was there to handle it when public wasn't enough.

I asked the AI to put together the [complete timeline](ai/t1c0). Every piece of evidence, every connection, chronological and cross-referenced. If I'm going to take this to anyone, it has to be airtight.

And then I asked about [prosecution](ai/l1a0). The answer wasn't encouraging.
