# The Albacore Club
date: [Wed, Jun 30 1937](1937-06-30)
tags: #place, #conspiracy

Exclusive gentlemen's club. Old money, old power. [Noah Cross](n1c0) is the founding member. Named after his favorite fish - the man brands everything.

The membership roster is the key to the [land grab](l1g0). The [cross-reference](ai/a1x0) with the [valley property deeds](ai/p1t0) is near-perfect. The "retirement homes," "church trusts," and elderly individual buyers on those deeds are all Albacore Club members or their estates. Cross isn't hiding behind strangers - he's hiding behind friends.

This is where the deals get made. Not city hall, not the council chambers. A private dining room where men with too much money decide what happens to a valley full of farmers who never stood a chance.

The club is the conspiracy's social infrastructure. Cross provides the capital and the plan. The members provide the names and the legal cover. Everyone gets rich when the [dam](a1v0) goes through.
