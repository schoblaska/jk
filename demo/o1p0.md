# Oak Pass Reservoir
date: [Fri, Jun 25 1937](1937-06-25)
tags: #place, #water

Municipal reservoir connected to the runoff channels [Mulwray's](h1m0) been inspecting at night. Locals say the water tastes wrong. Ranchers nearby say their wells turned bad about six months ago.

Kid at the reservoir told me straight out - "it tastes funny." Kids don't have theories, they just tell you what they notice.

Connected to the broader [drought pattern](d1r0). If someone is sabotaging the water supply to manufacture a crisis, this is one of the pressure points. Poison the local water, force the farmers to depend on the city supply, then let the city supply "fail."

Need to get a water sample tested. If there's something in it, that's physical evidence of sabotage.
