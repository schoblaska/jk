# The Scheme
date: [Fri, Jul 02 1937](1937-07-02)
tags: #theory, #conspiracy

The full picture. One machine, every part connected.

**The money play:**
- [Cross](n1c0) [engineers the drought](d1r0) - water dumped at ocean outfalls, wells poisoned in the valley
- Valley farmers go broke, sell land cheap
- [Albacore Club](a1c0) members buy through [shell companies and proxies](ai/p1t0) - 41 of 47 parcels traced back to the club
- [Alto Vallejo Dam](a1v0) gets pushed through city council with public money
- Water flows to Cross's land, values multiply tenfold

**The obstacle:**
- [Hollis Mulwray](h1m0) opposed the dam, knew about the [water discrepancies](ai/w1d0)
- Cross needed him discredited before he could go public
- [Ida Sessions](i1s0) hired to frame the affair, photos leaked to the [LA Times](ai/l1t0)
- When discrediting wasn't enough, Mulwray was [murdered](s1w0) - drowned in salt water, dumped in freshwater
- [Mulvihill](c1m0) handled the enforcement, [Escobar](l1e0) was pressured to close the case

**The personal:**
- Cross assaulted [Evelyn](e1c0) when she was fifteen. [Katherine](k1c0) is the child.
- Mulwray married Evelyn, helped hide Katherine from Cross
- Cross wants Katherine - told me to find her, offered money
- With Mulwray dead, Evelyn is the last barrier between Cross and the girl

The water and the girl. That's what it's always been about. Control. Cross lost the water in 1924 and he's taking it back. He lost access to Katherine and he's taking her back. The [conspiracy timeline](ai/t1c0) lays it all out.

The question isn't whether Cross is guilty. It's whether anyone in this city has the power to stop him.
