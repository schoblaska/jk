# Evelyn Mulwray
date: [Wed, Jun 23 1937](1937-06-23)
tags: #person, #cross-family

The real Mrs. Mulwray. Daughter of [Noah Cross](n1c0) - old money, the kind that built this city. Married [Hollis Mulwray](h1m0), which must have been interesting given her father and Mulwray's falling out over the water supply.

Met her today. She walked into my office ready to destroy me, but pivoted fast once she understood we'd both been played by [whoever hired Ida Sessions](i1s0). Smart enough to see the bigger picture. Hired me to find out who set this up.

Beautiful, composed, guarded. But when I mentioned [the girl](t1g0), something shifted - just for a second. She knows more than she's saying.

Moves in society circles but keeps a low profile for someone with that much family money. Not the type to make scenes, which makes the scene in my office all the more telling. She's scared of something beyond a scandal.
