# "She's My Sister"
date: [Wed, Jun 30 1937](1937-06-30)
tags: #mystery, #cross-family

That's what [Evelyn](e1c0) said when I pressed her about [the girl](t1g0). "She's my sister."

But her body said something else. The flinch before the words. Eyes dropping to the floor. Hands going still in her lap. That's not how you talk about a sister. That's how you talk about something you've rehearsed saying because the truth is worse.

The [background dossier](ai/e1b0) says Evelyn has no siblings on record. Her mother died young. If the girl is her sister, she's a half-sister nobody knows about - which means [Noah Cross](n1c0) and someone else, kept hidden.

Or Evelyn is lying entirely, and the girl is something else to her.

Either way: [Cross](n1c0) wants to find her. Evelyn wants to hide her. [Mulwray](h1m0) was protecting her. And now Mulwray is dead.

Whatever the truth is, it's bad enough that everyone around this girl either runs or dies.
