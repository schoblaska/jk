# Lt. Lou Escobar
date: [Sun, Jun 27 1937](1937-06-27)
tags: #person, #lapd

LAPD homicide lieutenant. Knows me from my time on the force - we both worked Chinatown. He's a decent cop, which in this city means a cop who knows which battles to pick and which to walk away from.

Warned me off the [Mulwray](h1m0) case at the reservoir. Not threatening - concerned. "It's not your case." But the way he said it told me everything. Someone above him wants this ruled accidental. Escobar knows it probably isn't, but he's not going to be the one to say so.

He won't help me, but he won't actively stop me either. As long as I don't make his life difficult. The moment I do, he'll come down on me because that's what the system requires.

A good man on a leash. I've known a few.
