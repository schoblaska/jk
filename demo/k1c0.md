# Katherine
date: [Fri, Jul 02 1937](1937-07-02)
tags: #person, #cross-family

[The girl](t1g0). She has a name now. Katherine.

[Evelyn's](e1c0) daughter. And [Noah Cross's](n1c0) daughter. Both at once. Cross assaulted Evelyn when she was fifteen. Katherine is the child of that. She's been hidden her entire life - raised in a quiet house in the valley, kept away from the Cross name, protected by Evelyn and by [Hollis Mulwray](h1m0).

The photos at Echo Park weren't an affair. Mulwray was visiting Katherine. The protective body language, the tenderness - he was the closest thing she had to a father. And now he's dead.

Cross wants her. Told me he wants to "make sure she's all right." That's not concern. That's ownership. He wants the last thing Evelyn has. He wants control, the way he's always wanted control - over the [water](d1r0), over the [land](l1g0), over his daughter, over his daughter's daughter.

Everyone around this girl either runs or dies. Evelyn ran. Mulwray died. I need to make sure Katherine doesn't become the next thing Cross takes.
