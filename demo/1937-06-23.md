# Wed, Jun 23 1937
tags: #journal

## Surveillance: Echo Park

Tailed [Mulwray](h1m0) again. This time he led me somewhere interesting - Echo Park. Met a young blonde woman there. They rented a rowboat, drifted around the lake. Intimate, but not the way you'd expect. More tender than romantic. She looks young. Very young. Maybe eighteen, maybe twenty.

Got the photographs. Good angles, clear faces. The job I was hired to do.

But something about it bothers me. He looked like a man carrying something heavy, not a man having an affair.

## The Photos Hit the Papers

Didn't even get a chance to deliver the photos to "Mrs. Mulwray." They're already on the front page of the LA Times. Someone else had a photographer there. Someone leaked the story before I could.

I didn't leak them. I don't work that way. Which means someone else was watching Mulwray at the same time I was. Someone who wanted this public, fast, before Mulwray or anyone else could control the narrative.

I've been used. The [LA Times angle](ai/l1t0) is worth pulling on.

## The Real Mrs. Mulwray

[Evelyn Mulwray](e1c0) - the *real* Evelyn Mulwray - walked into my office this afternoon with a lawyer. Poised, cold, furious. She never hired me. Never heard of me. Wants to know why a private detective is splashing her husband's personal life across every newsstand in the city.

The woman who hired me was a fake. [Ida Sessions](i1s0) - that's the name I'm working with. Someone put her up to playing Mrs. Mulwray. The question isn't who Mulwray was seeing. The question is who wanted the world to think he was cheating, and why they needed me to be the one holding the camera.

Evelyn threatened a lawsuit, but underneath the anger there's something else. Fear, maybe. She agreed to drop it if I find out who set us both up. She's my client now.
