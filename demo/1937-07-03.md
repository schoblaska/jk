# Sat, Jul 03 1937
tags: #journal

## The Plan

Simple. Get [Evelyn](e1c0) and [Katherine](k1c0) out of the city. Evelyn's housekeeper has a car. Drive to Ensenada, cross into Mexico, disappear. I just need to buy them a few hours - keep [Cross](n1c0) and the cops looking the wrong direction while they get clear.

I called [Escobar](l1e0) and fed him a line about new evidence at the reservoir. Sent him chasing his tail. Set up a meeting with Cross at the Biltmore to keep him occupied. Everything timed to the hour.

Evelyn packed one suitcase. Katherine didn't have much to pack.

The plan was to meet at Evelyn's housekeeper's place in Chinatown at 9 PM and hand them the car keys. Simple. Clean. The kind of plan that works when the world is fair.

## Chinatown

It went wrong.

[Escobar](l1e0) wasn't chasing his tail. Someone tipped him. He was waiting in Chinatown with four uniforms. Cross was there too - arrived with [Mulvihill](c1m0), calm as a Sunday morning, like he'd known the address all along.

Evelyn saw Cross and put Katherine in the car. She started the engine. Cross walked toward them. Evelyn told him to stay away. She said it twice.

Someone fired. I don't know if it was a cop or Mulvihill. It doesn't matter. The bullet went through the back of Evelyn's head. The car rolled forward and stopped against a fence. The horn was stuck.

Cross walked to the car. He took Katherine's hand. She was screaming. He pulled her out and held her against his chest and said something I couldn't hear. Mulvihill flanked them. They walked away.

The cops stood there. Escobar stood there. I stood there.

Nobody stopped him. Nobody was ever going to stop him.

## Aftermath

"Forget it, Jake. It's Chinatown."

That's what Walsh said. My associate. Standing behind me on the street while the horn was still blowing.

I've heard it before. When I was on the force, I tried to help someone in Chinatown. I made it worse. They told me the same thing then. You can't help. You don't understand enough. The system isn't yours to fix.

Evelyn deserved better. Katherine deserved better. [Mulwray](h1m0) deserved better.

The [water deal](s1f0) will go through. The [dam](a1v0) will be built. The [land](l1g0) will make Cross and his [club](a1c0) richer than they already are. And a girl who's been hidden her whole life just lost the only person who ever protected her.

I have a [complete record](w1k0). Every note, every connection, every piece of [evidence](ai/c1a0). It's all in here. A case file for a case nobody will prosecute.

I'm going to close this notebook. Not because it's finished. Because I can't look at it right now.
