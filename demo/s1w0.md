# Salt Water
date: [Sun, Jun 27 1937](1937-06-27)
tags: #evidence, #murder

[Mulwray](h1m0) drowned in salt water. His body was found in the [Oak Pass Reservoir](o1p0), which is freshwater.

He was killed somewhere near the ocean and moved. The outfall at Point Fermin - the one he'd been visiting at night to watch the [water dumping](d1r0) - is the obvious candidate. He went to gather evidence and someone was waiting for him. Just like [Mulvihill](c1m0) was waiting for me.

The [tidal analysis](ai/t1a0) puts the likely window between 1 and 4 AM on June 26. Kill him at the coast, drive the body to Oak Pass, dump it in the reservoir. By morning it looks like a drowning.

The salt water is the mistake. It proves the death scene was staged. But proving it to anyone who matters is another problem - [Escobar](l1e0) already wants this closed.
