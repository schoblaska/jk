# Claude Mulvihill
date: [Sun, Jun 27 1937](1937-06-27)
tags: #person, #suspect

The small one. The one who talked while the big one held the knife. Says he's "security" for the water department, but there's nothing water department about him. He's muscle. The kind they dress up with a title.

Former sheriff's deputy - the [dossier](ai/m1d0) confirmed that much, plus a termination for excessive force. Now he's at LADWP with no clear job description, which means his job is whatever someone tells him it is.

He knew I'd be at the [reservoir](o1p0). He was waiting. That means someone is tracking my movements, which means someone knows [Evelyn](e1c0) hired me.

The dossier ties him to [Noah Cross](n1c0)'s private security. That's the thread. Mulvihill doesn't freelance - he works for power, and Cross is the most powerful name in this whole mess.
