# Mon, Jun 21 1937
tags: #journal

## New Client

Woman walks in, says she's Mrs. Mulwray. Wants me to prove her husband Hollis is stepping out. Standard matrimonial work - tail the guy, get the photos, collect the check. She's well-dressed, composed, knows exactly what she wants. Almost too composed for a woman who thinks her husband's cheating.

Took the job. Easy money.

## Surveillance: City Hall

Followed Mulwray to a city council meeting downtown. Expected to catch him sneaking off to some love nest. Instead he's at the podium giving a speech about a dam.

The [Alto Vallejo Dam](a1v0) - some big reservoir project the council's pushing. Mulwray's dead set against it. Says the geology won't hold, references the [Van der Lip collapse](ai/v1d0) back in '28. Gets heated. Council members look uncomfortable. This is a man with enemies.

Not typical behavior for a cheating husband.

## Evening Notes

Something doesn't add up. [Mulwray](h1m0) is the chief engineer of the whole water department - a public figure, easy to find, easy to watch. Why hire a private detective? She could follow him herself. Or just ask around.

Filing it under "probably nothing." But I wrote it down, which means I don't believe that.
