# The Drought
date: [Fri, Jun 25 1937](1937-06-25)
tags: #water, #conspiracy

Official story: worst drought in decades. Water rationing across Los Angeles. Farmers in the San Fernando Valley losing crops, losing wells, losing everything.

But [Mulwray's](h1m0) midnight trips tell a different story. Freshwater is being dumped at the ocean outfalls after dark - thousands of gallons that should be in the reservoirs. Wells in the valley are being poisoned, not running dry. The water at [Oak Pass](o1p0) "tastes funny."

This isn't a drought. It's a siege. Someone is strangling the water supply to drive the valley into the ground. The [land purchases](ai/p1t0) started six months ago - right when the wells started going bad. That's not a coincidence.

The [LADWP numbers](ai/w1d0) confirm it: reservoir levels don't match what's being reported to the public. Water is disappearing from the books.
