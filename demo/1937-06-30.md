# Wed, Jun 30 1937
tags: #journal

## Lunch with Noah Cross

[Noah Cross](n1c0) invited me to his ranch in Pasadena. White fences, green grass, horses - the kind of spread that says "I own this state and I'm not hiding it." He cooked the fish himself. Albacore.

Charming. Avuncular. Terrifying. He wants me to find [the girl](t1g0) - the one in the photos with [Mulwray](h1m0). Says he's concerned for her welfare. "I just want to make sure she's all right, Mr. Gittes." Offered generous money.

I don't buy it. A man who wants to help a girl doesn't hire the PI his son-in-law's widow already hired. He wants leverage. Or possession.

He talked about Mulwray like a disappointment, not a murder victim. Said they had a "falling out over the future of this city." The [history](ai/c1h0) confirms it - Mulwray wanted the water public, Cross wanted it private. Cross lost. Men like Cross don't lose.

"Either you bring the water to LA, or you bring LA to the water." He said it like a joke. It isn't one.

## The Albacore Club

Cross mentioned it in passing - the name on the fish, the name on his club. I dug into the [Albacore Club](a1c0) after lunch. Exclusive gentlemen's outfit, very old money. And the membership list reads like a phone book of the [valley land purchases](ai/p1t0).

The [cross-reference](ai/a1x0) is almost perfect. The retirement homes, the church trusts, the elderly "buyers" - they're all Albacore Club members or connected to members. The shell game has a clubhouse. And Cross is the founding member.

He's not just behind the [land grab](l1g0). He built the machine that runs it.

## Evelyn's Secrets

Went back to [Evelyn](e1c0) to press her on [the girl](t1g0). She got agitated. More than agitated - frightened. Then she said it.

"She's my sister."

But the way she said it was wrong. The flinch before the words. The eyes going somewhere else. [She's lying](s1s0), or she's telling a truth that isn't the whole truth. Either way, the girl is the center of something Evelyn is desperate to protect.

Her father wants to find the girl. Evelyn wants to hide her. I'm standing in the middle and I don't know which side is safe.
