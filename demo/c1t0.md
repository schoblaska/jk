# Chinatown
date: [Sat, Jul 03 1937](1937-07-03)
tags: #place

It always comes back here.

When I was on the force, I was stationed in Chinatown. I tried to help a woman. I didn't understand enough about the situation, the people, the power. I made it worse. She got hurt. Maybe killed - I never found out. They transferred me out and told me to forget it.

Now it happened again. Different woman, same ending. [Evelyn](e1c0) is dead on a street in Chinatown because I thought I could outrun [Noah Cross](n1c0). I thought a plan and a car and a few hours' head start was enough to beat a man who owns this city.

Chinatown is the place where good intentions go to die. Where you learn that knowing the truth and being able to do something about it are two entirely different things. Where the distance between justice and power is measured in bodies.

I'll carry this one like I carry the last one. Filed under the things I can't fix and can't forget.
