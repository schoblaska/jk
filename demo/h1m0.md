# Hollis Mulwray
date: [Mon, Jun 21 1937](1937-06-21)
tags: #person, #water-department

Chief Engineer, Los Angeles Department of Water and Power. The man who decides where the water goes.

Public figure - testified before city council against the [Alto Vallejo Dam](a1v0). Says the geology is bad, says it'll be another [Van der Lip](ai/v1d0). Not a popular position with the development crowd.

Married to [Evelyn Mulwray](e1c0), née Cross. Her father is [Noah Cross](n1c0) - old money, old power. Mulwray and Cross used to be partners in the water business before it went public.

My client says she's his wife. Looking at the [public record](ai/m1p0), everything checks out on the surface. But a chief engineer fighting city hall over a dam project is more interesting than a cheating husband. Something about this doesn't add up.
