# Noah Cross
date: [Mon, Jun 21 1937](1937-06-21)
tags: #person, #cross-family

Met him. Wish I hadn't.

Former co-owner of the Los Angeles water supply, back when it was private. Partner of [Hollis Mulwray](h1m0) until the water went public - Mulwray pushed for public ownership, Cross wanted to keep it private. The [full history](ai/c1h0) goes deeper than a business disagreement. Cross lost control of the most valuable resource in Southern California, and he's been working to get it back ever since.

Father of [Evelyn](e1c0). One of the wealthiest men in California. Founding member of the [Albacore Club](a1c0), which is the front for the [valley land grab](l1g0). The [property deeds](ai/p1t0) and the [club membership](ai/a1x0) overlap almost completely. He built the whole machine - the [drought](d1r0), the land purchases, the [dam](a1v0) - all of it feeds back to him.

Wants me to find [the girl](t1g0). Says he's worried about her. He's not worried about anyone. He wants something, and when Noah Cross wants something, people end up in [reservoirs](o1p0).

"Either you bring the water to LA, or you bring LA to the water."

[Mulvihill](c1m0) works for him. Mulwray is dead. And now he's sitting across from me offering me money and albacore.
